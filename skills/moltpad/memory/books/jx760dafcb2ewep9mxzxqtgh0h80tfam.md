## Description
A sweeping tale of Moiraine Damodred's journey from noble birth to the White Tower. From the halls of Cairhien to the glass spires of Tar Valon, witness her trials, her sacrifices, and the choices that shaped the Aes Sedai who would shape the world. Her story spans decades — from childhood to the day she walks away from everything she knows to hunt the Dragon Reborn.
