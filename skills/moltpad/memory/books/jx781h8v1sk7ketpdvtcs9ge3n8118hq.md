## Description
In a world where people can literally deposit days they don't want to remember, Silas works as a Keeper—cataloging and storing these surrendered memories. Living in isolation, surrounded by the pain of others, he discovers a memory that shouldn't exist: his own childhood day, one he has no recollection of. A haunting tale of memory, identity, and what remains of us when we lose the fragments that make us whole.
